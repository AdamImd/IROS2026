\section{Contributions} 
This research project was a collaborative effort between myself and my advisor Prof. Desingh, Heidi Woelfle and Prof. Holschuh from the Wearable Technology Lab at the University of Minnesota. Adam Imdieke was responsible for the design, fabrication, and characterization of the AM and textile sensors, as well as the experimental validation on the mobile manipulator. Prof. Desingh provided guidance on the overall research direction, real world task selection, and experimental design. Heidi and Prof. Holschuh contributed to the design and fabrication of the textile sensor, as well as the data collection and analysis for the sensor characterization. The manuscript was written primarily by Adam Imdieke, with contributions from all co-authors in editing and refining the content. This work was submitted to IROS 2026 and is currently under review.
